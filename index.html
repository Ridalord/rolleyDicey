How to play Rolley Dicey

Objective: The objective of the game is to be the first player to reach the target score (default: 100 points).

Gameplay:

  • Each turn, a player rolls a dice by clicking the "Roll Dice" button.
  • The number rolled on the dice will be added to the player's current round score.
  • If the player rolls a 1, their current round score will be reset to 0, and it will be the next player's turn.
  • The player can choose to "Hold" their current round score by clicking the "Hold" button.
  • The current round score will be added to the player's total score.
  • If a player's total score reaches or exceeds the target score, they win the game.
  • To start a new game, click the "New Game" button.

Additional Features:

  • If a player rolls two consecutive 6s, their total score will be reset to 0.
  • The target score can be customized by entering a value in the "Target Score" input field.
